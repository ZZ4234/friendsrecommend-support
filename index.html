How do I add friends to my network?

Navigate to the Friends section in the app and search for friends by their username or email address. Send them a friend request to start sharing recommendations.

How do I send a recommendation?

Browse movies or TV shows, find something you love, tap the recommend button (üì§), and select which friends you'd like to send it to. They'll get notified instantly!

Why aren't I receiving recommendation notifications?

Check your device notification settings and ensure FriendsRecommend has permission to send notifications. Also verify you have an active internet connection.

How do I manage my watchlist?

When you receive a recommendation, tap "Add to Watchlist" to save it for later. Access your full watchlist from the main navigation menu.

Can I remove a recommendation from my list?

Yes! In your recommendations or watchlist, swipe left on any item to reveal delete options, or tap and hold for more actions.
